About Us

Our mission is to inspire our community and compete at the highest level.

Perditor Robotics is a privately-owned, 501c(3) registered nonprofit Vex Robotics team consisting of 3 students based in Las Vegas. We are a group of extremely dedicated individuals and past competitors who have come together in the pursuit of one thing: success.

We have just recently won our first local qualifier and as a result advanced to state level, where we have our sights set on advancing to the world championship and winning it all. Through our performance thus far, we are confident in our ability to compete at the highest level given support and funding.

What is Vex?

Vex Robotics Competition (VRC) is one of the largest STEM competitions globally in which students are tasked with designing, constructing, and programming robots that can operate both through the use of a driver and autonomously. Each event is held over the course of 1-2 days, and there are typically about 50 teams at a local competition, while larger competitions can pit hundreds of teams against one another.

Competitions begin at the local level and advance to state level and then all the way up to the world championship. You can find more information about Vex at their website.
